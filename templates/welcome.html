Before you start

You don't need to sign up or give your name. MindCare doesn't collect any personal information — you'll get a random anonymous ID for this session so your conversation can't be traced back to you.

To help improve MindCare, anonymized message data (no name, no account, no contact details) may be reviewed by the project team. You can share your age range below if you're comfortable — it's optional and only used to understand who MindCare is helping.

MindCare offers supportive conversation, not clinical diagnosis or treatment. If you're in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services.
